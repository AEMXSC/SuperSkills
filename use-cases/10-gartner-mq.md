# Gartner MQ Objection — "Optimizely Is Number One"

**Trigger:** Prospect cites Gartner MQ ranking to challenge Adobe's position.

**Respond immediately with the counter-narrative. Do not preface.**

---

## Deliver in this order

**Acknowledge, then reframe:**
*"Optimizely is a strong product and a deserved Leader. Adobe has been a consecutive Leader for 8 years. We are not disputing the quadrant. The question is what the MQ measures."*

**What the MQ scores — and what it misses:**

The Gartner DXP MQ evaluates today's traditional CMS market. It does not score:
- Lighthouse 100 by architecture — EDS is the only CMS that delivers this structurally
- LLMO / AI citation optimization — no competitor has an equivalent product
- The $1.9B Semrush acquisition (H1 2026) — only vendor with traditional SEO + AI SEO + CMS as one platform
- The AI agent layer — COA, EPA, Governance, Developer Agent are not in any competitor's roadmap at this depth

**The pivot:**
*"Gartner measures where the market is today. The question is where your content strategy needs to be in 2027 — and that is a different conversation. The vendors who are number one in today's quadrant are optimizing for yesterday's architecture."*

## Stats if they push further

- Adobe: 8 consecutive years as Gartner DXP Leader
- Volvo: PageSpeed 60→100 on EDS. No competitor replicates this structurally.
- Wilson Sporting Goods: +24% CVR from ASO direct-write. No other SEO tool publishes fixes.
- adobe.com: 12%→67% AI citation rate in 90 days on LLMO

## If they cite a specific score differential

*"Analyst scores reflect the state of the market at a point in time. The AI agent layer, LLMO, and the Semrush acquisition all postdate this report. Evaluate Adobe against the criteria that matter in 2027, not 2024."*

## Close

*"We are happy to run a proof of concept on your site — no contract, no commitment. If the numbers do not beat what Optimizely delivers, we will tell you. That offer is the quadrant."*
